Survival Hunter(생존)

The Survival Hunter is a brutal melee combatant, abandoning ranged weapons for closer combat, facing enemies eye-to-eye. Survival revolves around the core rotation of Serpent Sting, Kill Command, Raptor Strike and Wildfire Bomb. The core gameplay is to generate Focus with Kill Command while maintaining Serpent Sting and spending excess on Raptor Strike. This can get a bit more complex when using some of the talent combinations, but generally doesn't add extra buttons to your rotation.

Survival has unique Mastery. Not only does it increase your damage, but it also increases your survival with constant passive healing. However, it is important to remember that while Survival also heavily depends on pets like Beast Mastery, it is still unable to tame Exotics pets. Furthermore, you can use your Aspect of the Turtle to immune dangerous abilities, or Feign Death to make enemies lose interest in you.
